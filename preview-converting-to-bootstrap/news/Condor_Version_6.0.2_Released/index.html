Condor Version 6.0.2 Released

October 1, 1998

Associated Link

This is the new, stable version of Condor. It fully supports Solaris 2.5.1 and 2.6 on Intel and Sparc platforms, Linux for Intel (libc5 and glibc), SGI IRIX 6.2 through 6.4, Digital Unix 4.0, and HPUX 10.20.
