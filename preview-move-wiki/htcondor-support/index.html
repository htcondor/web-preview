HTCondor Support

Free Support for HTCondor

Free, community-based best effort support is available through the htcondor-users mailing list. HTCondor developers and users from around the world are subscribed to the list and volunteer their time and effort to answer questions and share information about HTCondor.

Additionally, the HTCondor Team can be contacted by sending email to htcondor-admin@cs.wisc.edu. Each message sent to this address recieves a tracking number, and the HTCondor team will try to resolve your issue. This is best-effort support and response times can vary between several minutes to several days, though the team does try and make initial contact by the next day. We thank you for your patience.

Please do not post a message to both htcondor-users and htcondor-admin at the the same time.

Fee-based Support for HTCondor

For users who require additional support for HTCondor beyond the free support listed above, there are several options.

The HTCondor Team provides fee-based support, and maintains a web page detailing the services provided.
